How RealStack Works

Seamless Asset Tokenization

Our platform simplifies the process of bringing real-world assets on-chain

1

Asset Onboarding

Submit your real-world asset for tokenization through our streamlined onboarding process. Our platform supports various asset classes including real estate, commodities, art, and financial instruments.

Asset Details

2

Legal Structure & Tokenization

Our platform creates the appropriate legal structure for your asset and generates compliant digital tokens that represent ownership rights. Smart contracts automate compliance, distributions, and governance.

Smart Contract

contract
AssetToken {
  string public name;
  uint256 public totalSupply;
  mapping(address => uint256) public balances;
}
3

Marketplace Listing

Your tokenized asset is listed on the RealStack marketplace, where investors can discover, analyze, and invest in it. Set your terms, minimum investment amounts, and distribution schedules.

Marketplace

4

Management & Distributions

Manage your tokenized asset through our intuitive dashboard. Automate distributions, communicate with investors, and track performance metrics in real-time.

Asset Dashboard
