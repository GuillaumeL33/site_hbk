About RealStack

Bridging the gap between traditional assets and blockchain technology

RealStack represents a strategic innovation in the digital asset space, designed to bridge the substantial gap between traditional physical assets and blockchain technology. Our platform enables the tokenization and trading of real-world assets (RWAs) with a specific focus on accessibility for retail investors, transparency in asset management, and community governance.

By launching on PumpFun, RealStack leverages an established crypto community while pioneering a model that combines the stability of physical assets with the liquidity benefits of tokenization.

Our Vision

The platform addresses key inefficiencies in both traditional asset markets and current tokenization efforts by implementing streamlined onboarding processes, fractional ownership mechanisms, and robust verification protocols.

We believe in democratizing access to real-world assets, making it possible for anyone to invest in commercial real estate, small businesses, and premium collectibles that were previously accessible only to institutional investors or high-net-worth individuals.

Market Opportunity

The tokenized real-world asset market represents a significant yet underdeveloped segment of the broader digital asset ecosystem. Traditional RWAs (including real estate, small businesses, and premium collectibles) represent a global market valued at over $300 trillion, yet less than 0.1% has been brought on-chain.

RealStack is positioned to capitalize on three converging trends:

  • Increasing retail investor demand for inflation-resistant assets with tangible backing
  • Growing frustration with the illiquidity of traditional alternative investments
  • Maturing blockchain infrastructure capable of supporting compliant asset tokenization

Our research indicates a potential addressable market of approximately $15 billion in the next three years, focusing specifically on assets valued between $500,000 and $10 million—a segment traditionally underserved by both conventional financial institutions and existing tokenization platforms.
